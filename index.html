Research

Affiliation

I am presently living and working in the beautiful city of Rijeka, Croatia. I am working as an experimental solid-state/condensed matter physicist in Laboratory for Surface and Materials Science, which is a joint laboratory of University of Rijeka Department of Physics and Centre for Micro and Nano Sciences and Technologies located in University departments building.

Experience

Application of synchrotron radiation and X-ray Photoemission Spectoscopy (XPS) techniques on semiconductor materials. Acquainted and worked on several electron spectroscopy methods at the National Synchrotron Radiation Research Centre in Hsinchu, Taiwan.
